Arabian Fried Chicken Mandi

I love Arabian Fried Chicken Mandi because of its rich spices and fragrant rice and amazing taste.

How to Make Arabian Fried Chicken Mandi

  1. Marinate Chicken
    • Mix chicken with yogurt, lemon juice, turmeric, chili powder, cumin, and salt.
    • Let it rest for 2 hours or overnight.
  2. Cook Rice
    • Heat ghee, add whole spices, then saut√© basmati rice.
    • Add water or broth, cover, and cook on low heat.
  3. Fry Chicken
    • Deep-fry marinated chicken until golden brown and crispy.
  4. Smoke the Rice (Optional)
    • Place hot charcoal in the rice pot, add ghee, and cover for 5 minutes.
  5. Serve
    • Spread rice on a plate, place chicken on top, and garnish with fried onions, almonds, and raisins.
